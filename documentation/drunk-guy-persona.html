Drunk User Persona

Name

Theodore Mckinnely III

Age

21

Profession

Full time student pursuing masters in computer science. In a fraternity at ASU in his junior year.

Technology

Linux power user. Has a Dell Alienware Aurora R12 with Kali Linux OS and two 14 inch monitors. Also has an apple iphone 13 pro max with iOS. Uses google chrome for everything.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Absolutely slammed with homework and frat parties, doesn't want to spend time arguing over trivial things like where to eat. Likes to find the latest and greatest apps for everything, especially apps that help make decisions as a group.

Frustrations and Needs

Theo needs some simple automation in his life without removing choice. He doesn't care where he and his friends decide to eat, only that he wants takeout and he wants it fast.

Goals

When he's drunk at 2:30 am and trying to order food, anything that will eliminate an argument and save time is exactly what he and his frat needs. A quick meal will settle his bubble guts and reduce the severity of any possible hangover.

User Story

As a drunk user, me and the boys need to get a late night fourth meal without spending time arguing.

Use-Case and Interaction Flow

Use-Case

User SpaghettiYeti and his buddies need food at 3 am. They can never decide where to eat diplomatically, so they found a simple app that will help them decide quickly.

Precondition

User SpaghettiYeti logs into his Restaurant Tinder account/ User SpaghettiYeti opens the Restaurant Tinder app.

Post-condition

The winning restaurant has been chosen.

    Interaction Flow
  • RT app asks Theo for a zip code.
  • Theo types in zip code and pushes big continue arrow button.
  • RT finds 9 restaurants in the area that are open and deliver at this time. RT shows different types of food to choose from e.g. chinese, pizza, any, etc.
  • Theo selects any.
  • RT shows Theo the first choice with an option to swipe right or swipe left.
  • Theo quickly swipes through all the choices.
  • RT tells Theo "tallying votes" while the other members finish swiping.
  • Theo tells the guys to hurry up.
  • RT shows all members the winning restaurant.
