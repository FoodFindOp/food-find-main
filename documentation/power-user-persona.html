Persona

Profession

Name: Madeline

Age: 26

Works full time at a local tech company, has a bachelors degree in CS from the University of Florida.

Technology

Mac power user. Has a 2021 MacBook Air with new ios 15 installed. Also has the new Iphone 13 with ios 15.1. Uses safari as her go-to search engine.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Busy with work during the week and works on side projects during the weekend. Likes to save time by downloading apps to help automate her life. Keeps a routine and rarely breaks it, she has a tight schedule yet is great at distributing her time effectively.

Frustrations and Needs

Madeline is really needing help automating deciding where and what to eat. She knows she wastes time by taking a while to decide what to eat and is looking to save that extra time. She still wants to choose where to eat but is looking for a quick and easy app that helps her make a decision faster.

Goals

Whether Madeline is on her lunch break or out with friends she wants to be able to save her time by deciding where her or her and her friends go to eat. So that way she will have more time to either relax or get more things done faster.

User Story

As a power user, I really need a faster way to more efficiently decide on what to eat so that I can allocate my saved time to more things I need to get done.

User Case

User is looking for something to eat but doesn't know what to eat, so she downloads RT to help her automate picking a place to eat.

Pre-Condition

User pulls up phone and downloads RT.

Post-Condition

User found an app that helps her decides on what to eat.

Interaction Flow:

  • User is hungry and can't decide on what to eat
  • User finds RT (restaurant tinder) downloads and opens the app.
  • App opens to the home screen, app asks "create group?", "join a group?", or "flying solo?"
  • User clicks on "flying solo"
  • RT goes to swiping screen and 12 restaurants show on screen
  • User decides on 2 restaurants
  • RT automatically randomizes and chooses a restaurant between the two choices
  • User figured out what place to eat at after using app
